KLEYNOD SOFT COMPANY IS OFFERING INTEGRATED WEB PROMOTION SERVICE

Work on search engine promotion includes internal and external site optimization activities aimed at improving the position of each of the promoted searches.

WE OFFER:

1. SEO-optimization and web site promotion

1.1. Complete SEO-site audit:

  • Verification of the validity of the code, fixing bugs;
  • Introduction of meta tags (title, keywords; goods for photos in the catalogue - alt, title);
  • Work with headings (H1, H2);
  • Menu: structure formation breadcrumbs;
  • The availability and correctness of the file robots.txt, overall assessment of pages in terms of their suitability for indexing by search engines;
  • Analysis of a site's visibility in search engines;
  • Analysis of the link structure of a website, validating links;
  • Analysis of traffic sources;
  • Analysing of the position in the search results of sites with the similar subjects.

1.2. SEO-promotion site:

  • Analysis of key phrases;
  • Formation of an unfolded map of the site;
  • Formation of promotion strategy (low-frequency / high-demand);
  • Creating a semantic core for a site, the allocation of an array of keywords, which will be the promotion;
  • Filling the site with unique content in accordance with a set of keywords if enabled (see Optimizing your site structure, work with content);
  • Registration site in directories, posting (purchase) of references to thematic resources, forums and blogs;
  • Add the site to DMOZ, DMOZ, etc.

2. Optimizing your site structure, work with content

2.1. Analysis of the structure of the site:

  • Assessment of the overall logic structure of the site;
  • Optimization of the structure of the catalogue;
  • Assessment of the completeness of information on potential "landing" page (home page, a page describing popular products catalogue. Page with a description of the application, etc.)

2.2. Analysis of usability:

  • Analysis of convenience and completeness of the navigation system;
  • Overall assessment of convenience interfaces;
  • Quality control display the site in different browsers;
  • Testing the usability and correctness of the registration form, ordering, payment, feedback;
  • The existence of mechanisms to find information on the site, the correctness of their work.

2.3. Working with content:

  • Analysis of the main page of the site in terms of saturation necessary content, the logic of his filing, readability, etc.;
  • Analysis of the completeness of the user information (Section "About us", "Delivery", "Contacts");
  • Analysis of the completeness of the information on the page (product description, abstract, quotations, design titles, keyword saturation, the introduction of keywords in headlines, quotes, links);
  • Analysis of the overall readability of the text;
  • Development of a single scheme of visual text formatting (headings, paragraphs, quotes, links, etc.);
  • Internal relinking pages (links to similar products on the catalogue pages, links to the promotional materials);
  • Partitioning with unique content, content writing, in accordance with the semantic nucleus formed in the process of SEO-site analysis (options sections: information about the authors, selected chapters from popular books, reference, etc.).

3. Web site promotion

3.1. The standard means of promotion:

  • Contextual advertising: Google Adsense, Yandex. Creation of advertising campaigns in accordance with the generated set of key phrases;
  • Ads on topical resources: placing banners and other types of advertising media on topical resources (legal, educational topics, etc.);
  • Publication of articles, press releases, announcements on the thematic resources, forums and blogs.

3.2. Promotion in social networks

  • Ensure that the mere presence in social networks - that is, create profiles on the most popular of them and update them on information from a predefined intervals - to place announcements of publications publish quotes, useful facts, and so on;
  • Create and implement a strategy of active promotion via social networks.
