Digital Archive of Documents

"Kleynod Soft" Ltd. offers a special service -

creation of an electronic archive of documents

for safety purpose and with the possibility of further working with documents in electronic form.

Electronic archive simplifies and automatize of workflow processes.

We offer a full range of services for creating electronic archives, directories, libraries with centralized or local access rights, and separation of rights in respect of content, process and information use.

The process of creating an electronic archive

predicts the implementation of all project phases:

  • Preparation of technical specifications;
  • Development of algorithm and rights of access to information;
  • Works on crawling and indexing materials;
  • Development of software;
  • Development of user-friendly interface and;
  • Development of user guidelines.


Electronic archive – it is ...


  • Increasing of productivity with costs reducing;
  • Preservation of original documents;
  • Exemption of physical space to store documents with decreasing costs of copying and shipping documents in paper form;
  • Quickly finding the required information;
  • Segmentation of documents and extensive filtering them in future work;
  • Setting permissions for use and handling of documents;
  • All operations on documents in one centralized resource, organizing access to a common database of documents for organizations that have separate branches;
  • Additional saving documents on remote resource (such as cloud hosting) that ensures protection from damage and to restore basic resources as needed;
  • Ability to constant replenishment of new and existing processing documents;
  • Automatic monitoring of retention periods;
  • Using documents routing;
  • The use of digital signature;
  • Statistics on all processes with the archive;
  • Tracking "life cycle" of each document.

Electronic document management system

– is a single networking platform where all information is stored and any authorized officer of the organization can easily and quickly access the information is need.

The use of electronic document management system enhances the quality and efficiency of working with documents, and will reduce operating costs related to the organization of communication within the company.
