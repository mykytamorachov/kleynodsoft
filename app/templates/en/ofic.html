For Offices and Logistic Centres

Our system is indispensable for office centers, logistic complexes, schools, businesses, etc.

Our

solution will help to minimize the human resources and provide a guaranteed, instant, and documented notification

of officer on-duty of the fact of unauthorized access of the object or area that is protected.

Our system:

  • Forms the database of tenants (customers, clients);

  • Make documented records of a fact of unauthorized access to an object or area (photos) ;

  • Inform of the officer on-duty by the audible alarm and documented (send photos);

  • Providing regular service detailed information about the facility and the owner (user) ;

  • Does not require constant object monitoring by surveillance operator;

  • Protects the database and documented information from unauthorized use or destruction;

  • Provides long-term preservation of databases and documented information.

Our System is automated and eliminates the human influence as a negative factor of its correct and efficient operation.

Our System can work with alarm system or separately.

Our System of video recording and alerts help your customers in a conversation with insurance companies and law enforcement agencies - quickly find the offender.
